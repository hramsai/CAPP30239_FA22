Reading Police Killings in 2015

The data used for these charts come from the Washington Post.

At the outset, a Racial Distribution of the Killings:

As the chart shows, 26% of the deceased are Black. It also shows that over 50% of those killed are 'White'. It is important, however, it note that the population distribution tells a different story.

The Populations Distribution

As the chart shows, only 13% of the population identifies as 'Black'. This shows that the killings are disproportionately of black people.

Number of Killings Across the Year

This chart shows the distribution of total killings through the months of 2015. As is evident, we see a slight dip in the summer months, and an increase in July and August.

Distribution of How People were Killed

As the chart shows, over 1400 of the killings were through shooting, while 58 were through a taser.
